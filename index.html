Controls & Rules

Movement

  • W, A, S, D or Arrow Keys: Move
  • Swipe: Move (Mobile/Touch)

Actions

  • F or Shoot Btn: Shoot Arrow (kills Wumpus in direction facing)
  • G or Grab Btn: Grab Item (Gold, Potion, Map, etc.)
  • C or Climb Btn: Climb out (Only at the start cell, must have Gold to win)

Items (Passive)

  • 🔥 Torch: Automatically increases your vision radius.
  • 🧵 Rope: Automatically saves you if you fall into a pit.
  • 🧪 Potion: Automatically revives you if eaten by the Wumpus.
  • 🗺️ Map: Read immediately upon grabbing to reveal 3 safe rooms.
